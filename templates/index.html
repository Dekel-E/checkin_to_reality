About Gap Scores

Gap Score measures the difference between user reviews and our predicted reality score. A higher gap score indicates that the hotel's actual experience may differ significantly from its reviews.

  • Low Risk (0-30): Reviews likely match reality
  • Medium Risk (30-70): Some discrepancies expected
  • High Risk (70-100): Significant differences from reviews
